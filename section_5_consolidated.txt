SECTION 5: ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA BY SURFACE (CONSOLIDATED)

This section uses a reference-based model: POS (§5.3) contains the comprehensive specification for all common patterns. Subsequent surfaces reference POS and note deltas only.

================================================================================
5.3 POS (In-Person Terminal) 
================================================================================

This section serves as the reference specification for payment method selection, receipt structure, manual entry, and disclosure patterns used across all surfaces.

---
5.3.1 CATALOG-BASED PRODUCT SELECTION
---

Product Pricing in Catalog:
a) Auto-Calculate ON:
   •	System stores cash/ACH price in catalog
   •	List price calculated as: cash price ÷ (1 - discount%) with rounding

b) Auto-Calculate OFF:
   •	System stores list price in catalog
   •	Cash discount calculated as: list price × discount%

Cart / Pre-Checkout:
   •	Display list prices
   •	Show "Cash/ACH option available" indicator

---
5.3.2 PAYMENT METHOD SELECTION 
---

Customer-Facing Display Must Show:
   •	💳 Card: $XXX.XX
   •	💵 Cash: $XXX.XX (Save $X.XX)

Merchant-Facing Display Shows:
   •	Same information as customer-facing

Flow:
   •	Customer must confirm payment method selection before proceeding
   •	Selected method locks in final price

---
5.3.3 RECEIPT STRUCTURE
---

Receipt (Card Payment):
   •	Show items at List price
   •	Subtotal
   •	Tax (calculated on subtotal)
   •	Total
   •	Payment Method: CREDIT CARD or DEBIT CARD
   •	No discount line (card customers pay full price)

Receipt (Cash/ACH Payment):
   •	Show items at card/list price (NOT discounted price per item)
   •	Subtotal
   •	Tax (calculated on subtotal)
   •	Cash/ACH Discount (X%): -$X.XX
   •	Total (subtotal + tax - discount)
   •	Payment Method: CASH or ACH
   •	Footer text: "Thank you! You saved $X.XX with cash/ACH payment."

---
5.3.4 DISCLOSURES
---

Physical Signage Required:
   •	Must be posted at entry and point of sale
   •	Example text: "List prices are for card payments. Save X% when you pay with cash or ACH."
   •	Signage requirement documented in onboarding materials

Receipt Disclosure:
   •	Cash/ACH receipts must show discount as separate line item
   •	Card receipts show no discount language

Verbal Disclosure (Virtual Terminal):
   •	Merchant must verbally disclose pricing to customer during phone order
   •	System displays reminder: "Before taking payment, inform customer: Card price is $XXX.XX, Cash/ACH price is $XXX.XX"

5.5 INVOICING
================================================================================

Payment Method Selection & Receipts: Same as POS §5.3.2 and §5.3.3

Invoice-Specific Requirements:

---
5.5.1 INVOICE CREATION (C1) — MULTI-ITEM ENTRY
---

Line Item Entry:
   •	Merchant enters multiple items with:
     - Description
     - Quantity
     - Price per item (cash price or list price based on auto-calculate setting)
   •	System calculates list price or cash discount per §5.3.1 logic

Invoice Preview (Before Sending):
   •	Shows line items with quantities and prices
   •	Subtotal (card/list price)
   •	Tax (calculated on subtotal)
   •	Cash/ACH Discount: -$X.XX
   •	Total shown both ways: Card $XXX.XX / Cash/ACH $XXX.XX

Send Invoice:
   •	Delivery: Email and/or SMS with payment link
   •	Subject line and body text: Configurable by merchant
   •	Must include: "Card and Cash/ACH payment options available"

---
5.5.2 INVOICE PAYMENT PAGE (C2)
---

Invoice View (Email, PDF, Web Page):
   •	Display line items at card/list price
   •	Subtotal
   •	Tax
   •	Total: Card $XXX.XX / Cash/ACH $XXX.XX (both shown)
   •	Explanation text: "Pay with cash or ACH and save X%"

Payment Method Selection:
   •	💳 Card (Credit or Debit): $XXX.XX
   •	🏦 ACH / Bank Transfer: $XXX.XX (Save $X.XX)
   •	Customer must select payment method before entering payment details
   •	Confirmation screen shows final amount

Receipt:
   •	Same structure as POS §5.3.3
   •	Delivered via email/SMS
   •	Payment Method: CARD or ACH

Disclosures:
   •	Invoice must state payment options and discount clearly
   •	Receipt must show discount as separate line item for cash/ACH payments

5.6 PAYLINKS
================================================================================

Payment Method Selection & Receipts: Same as POS §5.3.2 and §5.3.3

Paylink-Specific Requirements:

---
5.6.1 PAYLINK CREATION (C1) — SINGLE-ITEM ENTRY
---

Creation Interface:
   •	Merchant enters:
     - Amount (cash price or list price based on auto-calculate setting)
     - Description (optional)
   •	System calculates card/cash prices and displays preview:
     - "Card customers pay: $XXX.XX"
     - "Cash/ACH customers pay: $XXX.XX (save $X.XX)"

Paylink URL:
   •	Sharable via SMS, email, social media, QR code
   •	URL loads payment page (see §5.6.2)

---
5.6.2 PAYLINK PAYMENT PAGE (C2)
---

Display:
   •	Description (if provided)
   •	Amount: Card $XXX.XX / Cash/ACH $XXX.XX
   •	Explanation: "Save X% when you pay with cash or ACH"

Payment Method Selection:
   •	💳 Card: $XXX.XX
   •	🏦 ACH: $XXX.XX (Save $X.XX)
   •	💵 Cash (if in-person pickup/delivery enabled): $XXX.XX (Save $X.XX)
   •	Customer selects before entering payment details

Receipt:
   •	Same structure as POS §5.3.3
   •	Digital delivery (email optional)

Disclosures:
   •	Payment page must clearly show both payment options and savings
   •	Receipt must show discount line for cash/ACH payments

5.7 VIRTUAL TERMINAL / MANUAL ENTRY
================================================================================

Entry Screen:
a) Amount Input:
   •	Label changes based on auto-calculate setting:
     - Auto-calculate ON: "Enter Cash/ACH Price"
     - Auto-calculate OFF: "Enter List Price"
   •	Physical keypad interface for amount entry
   •	Input field: $____.__

b) Real-Time Calculation Display:
   •	If auto-calculate ON: "List price (with rounding): $XXX.XX"
   •	If auto-calculate OFF: "Cash/ACH price (after X% discount): $XXX.XX"

c) Preview Before Proceeding:
   •	Shows: "Customer will see: Card $XXX.XX / Cash $XXX.XX"
   •	Merchant confirms before proceeding to checkout

Payment Processing:
   •	Proceeds directly to payment method selection (§5.3.2)
   •	Receipt generated per §5.3.3 pattern
   •	Single-use entry (clears after transaction)



================================================================================
5.8 POST-TRANSACTION MANAGEMENT
================================================================================

(This section remains unchanged from original PRD — applies universally across all surfaces)

Refunds:
   •	If cash discount was applied: refund must reverse discount
   •	Refund receipt shows original payment method and discount reversal
   •	Example: Original $100 cash payment with $3 discount → Full refund shows $100 refunded

Voids:
   •	Must void at original amount (before or after discount based on payment method)
   •	Void receipt shows original transaction details

Partial Refunds:
   •	Discount proportionally adjusted based on refund amount
   •	Example: $100 order with $3 cash discount, $50 partial refund → $1.50 discount reversed

Transaction History / Reporting:
   •	Must show:
     - List price (card price)
     - Cash/ACH discount amount (if applicable)
     - Final amount charged
     - Payment method
   •	Filters: Payment method, date range, discount applied Y/N

Settlement Reporting:
   •	Cash/ACH transactions settled at discounted amount
   •	Card transactions settled at list price
   •	Report must separate card vs cash/ACH volume for merchant reconciliation
