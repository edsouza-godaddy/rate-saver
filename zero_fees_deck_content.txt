SLIDE 5: WHAT WE'RE BUILDING (may need 2 slides if content is dense)
================================================================================

TITLE: What We're Building

SUBTITLE: Two Models — Merchant Choice

MODEL A: CREDIT FEE
Approach: Lower posted prices + surcharge on credit only
✓ Strengths: Familiar approach, attractive shelf prices, Rate Saver continuity
✗ Limitations: Debit gap, state restrictions, consumer backlash risk

MODEL B: EMBEDDED + DISCOUNT
Approach: Higher posted prices + cash/ACH discount
✓ Strengths: True zero fees (credit+debit), no state restrictions, no surprise fees
✗ Limitations: Higher posted prices, requires merchant trust in pricing help

KEY MESSAGE:
Merchant decides which model fits their business (or channel-based mix)

THE PRODUCT:
• Compliant cash discounting / dual pricing platform
• All surfaces at launch: POS, Invoicing, Paylinks, Online
• Guided setup with pricing calculation help
• Exceptional experience: setup, customer UX, staff operability, transparency

ASSETS READY:
• Full comparison documentation
• Prototype mocks (8 scenarios: setup, economics, staff training, disclosures)
• Technical foundation from Dough Phase 1
