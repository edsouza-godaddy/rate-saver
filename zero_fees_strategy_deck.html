    What We're Building

    Two Models — Merchant Choice

    Model A: Credit Fee

    Lower posted prices + surcharge on credit only
    Strengths

    Familiar approach, attractive shelf prices, Rate Saver continuity

    Limitations

    Debit gap, state restrictions, consumer backlash risk

    Model B: Embedded + Discount

    Higher posted prices + cash/ACH discount
    Strengths

    True zero fees (credit+debit), no state restrictions, no surprise fees

    Limitations

    Higher posted prices, requires merchant trust in pricing help

    Merchant decides which model fits their business (or channel-based mix)

    The Product

  • Compliant cash discounting / dual pricing platform
  • All surfaces at launch: POS, Invoicing, Paylinks, Online
  • Guided setup with pricing calculation help
  • Exceptional experience: setup, customer UX, staff operability, transparency
