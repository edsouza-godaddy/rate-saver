What Would Violate Transparency?
❌ Non-Compliant Examples
  • Showing $100 on shelf, $103 at checkout with NO fee disclosure
  • Changing price after card type is detected ("oh it's credit, add $3")
  • Advertising $100 everywhere but charging $103 without explanation
  • Surcharging debit cards (illegal)
  • Applying fee only AFTER customer swipes/taps
✓ Compliant Practices
  • Clear signage at entry/menu that fee or discount applies
  • Price displayed for each payment method before selection
  • Receipt shows breakdown (base, fee/discount, total)
  • Consistent language across all touchpoints
  • Staff trained to explain pricing clearly
